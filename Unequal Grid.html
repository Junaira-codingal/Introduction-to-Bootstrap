Zero Waste Tips

Reducing Plastic Use
  • Carry a reusable water bottle instead of buying plastic ones.
  • Use cloth bags instead of plastic bags while shopping.
  • Switch to a bamboo toothbrush instead of a plastic one.
  • Say no to plastic straws and use stainless steel or paper alternatives.
  • Buy in bulk to avoid unnecessary plastic packaging.
Composting (Turning Waste into Fertilizer)
  • Start a home compost bin for food scraps and garden waste.
  • Learn what can and can't be composted (e.g., avoid meat and dairy).
  • Composting reduces landfill waste and creates natural fertilizer.
Upcycling Ideas (Reusing Old Items Creatively)
  • Turn old glass jars into storage containers or plant pots.
  • Make eco-friendly tote bags from old T-shirts.
  • Use worn-out clothes to create reusable cleaning cloths.
  • Repurpose old furniture instead of throwing it away.
